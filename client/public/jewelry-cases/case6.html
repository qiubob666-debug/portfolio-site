Solar Technology

Powered by Light

Our solar cells convert any light — sunlight, office lighting, even a candle — into energy that powers your watch. No battery changes. No waste. Just time.

1

Light Absorption

Invisible solar cells beneath the dial capture photons from any light source.

2

Energy Conversion

Photons are converted to electrical energy and stored in a rechargeable cell.

3

6-Month Reserve

A full charge provides up to 6 months of power, even in complete darkness.

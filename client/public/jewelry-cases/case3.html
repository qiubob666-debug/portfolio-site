Our Process

From Atom to Atelier

Every AETHER diamond begins as a tiny carbon seed, grown over weeks in a state-of-the-art reactor, then hand-cut and set by master artisans.

01

Seed Selection

A microscopic diamond seed is placed in a plasma reactor at 1,500°C, replicating Earth's natural process.

02

Growth

Over 2-4 weeks, carbon atoms crystallize layer by layer, forming a rough diamond identical to mined stones.

03

Cutting

Master cutters shape each stone to maximize brilliance, fire, and scintillation using precision laser technology.

04

Setting

Each diamond is hand-set in recycled precious metals by our atelier craftspeople in Los Angeles.

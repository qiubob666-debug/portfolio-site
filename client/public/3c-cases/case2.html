Our Mission

The sky is not the limit.
It's just the beginning.

"Every frame shot from above tells a story that ground-level cameras never could. We built SKYVEX to give that perspective to everyone."

SKYVEX was born in a garage in Shenzhen in 2016 by three aerospace engineers who believed that professional aerial cinematography shouldn't require a helicopter. Today, SKYVEX drones are used by filmmakers in 140 countries, from Hollywood blockbusters to independent documentaries. The APEX 4K represents our most ambitious engineering achievement: a drone that thinks like a cinematographer, flies like a bird, and fits in a backpack.

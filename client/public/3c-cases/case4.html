Our Philosophy

Sound is emotion.

"We don't just reproduce sound. We recreate the feeling of being in the room where the music was made."

RESONIQ was founded by acoustic engineers who believed that headphones had gotten too complicated and not good enough at the one thing that mattered: sound. Since 2009, we have focused obsessively on two things — eliminating noise and perfecting audio reproduction. The SILENCE PRO is the result of 16 years of acoustic research, 847 prototype iterations, and feedback from over 3,000 audiophiles, musicians, and everyday listeners. It is not just a headphone. It is a portal to pure sound.

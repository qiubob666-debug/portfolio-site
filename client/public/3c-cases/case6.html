— our philosophy

form follows
function.
always.

FORMWERK was founded in Stockholm in 2012 by two industrial designers and one sound engineer who were frustrated with synthesizers that were either too complex to use or too simple to be interesting. The TX-1 is the result of three years of iteration — 47 physical prototypes, 200+ hours of musician feedback sessions, and an obsessive commitment to removing every unnecessary element. What remains is pure instrument. Every knob does exactly one thing. Every button is exactly where your hand expects it. The sound is the point. Everything else is noise.
